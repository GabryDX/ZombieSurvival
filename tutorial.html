TUTORIAL

Get ready to kill as many enemies as possible before the timer expires! Use:

  • the buttons W-A-S-D to move in the field
  • the space bar to shot
  • the mouse pointer to direct the shot.
Remember that if the enemies hits you they will make you change position.
You can also produce a continuous stream of shots pressing and kept press the space bar.
Pay attention! Enemies can appear everywhere!
