TUTORIAL

How to move and to kill enemies? Use the mouse to direct the visual and:

  • the buttons W A S D to move in the field;
  • the space bar or left mouse click to shoot;
  • right mouse click to zoom if you need to aim;
  • the SHIFT button to run;
Zombies appear randomly in the map. Hence, pay attention and don't let
them get too close or you will die!


Hint: keep the space bar pressed!
